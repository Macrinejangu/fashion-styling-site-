Our Philosophy

At The Syle Sankofa, we believe in dressing with purpose, memory, and soul. "Sankofa" - a word from the Akan people of Ghana - teaches us to retrieve what was once lost and bring it forward into the now. We apply this athos to fashion by combininb ancestral grace with modern edge.
